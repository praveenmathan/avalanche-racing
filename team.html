FAZAL KHAN

OWNER AND TEAM PRINCIPAL

Fazal Khan, a Mechanical Engineer is a Champion racer and participates in multiple categories of racing on offer in India. He was one of the top 5 Racing and Karting Champions of India (1999 to 2007). Hailing from Bangalore, he has participated and raced in all forms of Kart’s from 4-stroke to 2-stroke and raced in all forms of Indian single seater Formula racing Cars from FISSMI in 2000 to FLGB 2005, Formula Hyundai to Formula Ford F1600 2013.

Fazal has 13 years of racing experience, 9 Years of experience in running a Karting Team and 8 years of experience in training drivers in Racing and Karting. The combination of his dedication, experience and perseverance towards striving for excellence has developed him into a master in Training and Team Management.
