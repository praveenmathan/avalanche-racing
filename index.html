OUR STORY

AVALANCHE MOTORSPORTS is one of the leading formula Racing teams and Motorsports Management enterprise of India Started in the year 2002 as a Professional Karting Team, we have a long standing history in the Indian Motorsports scene starting in Karting with many accolades in National Championship’s and Endurance races.

The Team debuted into their formula cars in the FLGB F4 class in the year 2013 and have since made a mark in the Indian National Racing Championship with multiple podiums and accolades over six years of team buildup . The best racing drivers of India have been a part of the team over the years and the rookies get training and support in our Racing Academy . We were National Champions of year 2017 in the F4 class.

Avalanche Motorsports

OUR MISSION

With a commendable performance and presence in the JK TYRE FMSCI National Racing Championship for the last five years and with consecutive technical team buildup, our mission is the pursuit of excellence in providing the best motorsports management in India and setting higher standards for ourselves and striving constantly to achieve it. Our aim is to train youngsters from level zero to Karting to Racing and guide & promote them in National and International level.
