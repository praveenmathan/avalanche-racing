OUR STORY

AVALANCHE RACING is one of the leading formula Racing teams and Motorsports Management enterprise of India Started in the year 2002 as a Professional Karting Team, we have a long standing history in the Indian Motorsports scene starting in Karting with many accolades in National Championship’s and Endurance races.

The Team entered Formula Racing with their FLGB Formula 4 (F4) Cars in 2013, and have made a mark in the JK TYRE FMSCI National Racing Championship with multiple podiums and accolades over 6 years. The best of the top racing drivers of India have been part of the team. Rookies get trained and get support for their racing careers in our Racing Academy. We were the National Champions of 2017.

Avalanche Racing

OUR MISSION

With a commendable performance and presence in the JK TYRE FMSCI National Racing Championship for the last five years and with consecutive technical team buildup, our mission is the pursuit of excellence in providing the best motorsports management in India and setting higher standards for ourselves and striving constantly to achieve it. Our aim is to train youngsters from level zero to Karting to Racing and guide & promote them in National and International level.
